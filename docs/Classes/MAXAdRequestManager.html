MAXAdRequestManager

public class MAXAdRequestManager: NSObject

Use a MAXAdRequestManager to coordinate refreshing static ad units (banners) in the following circumstances: 1) Auto-refresh periodically (e.g. every 30 seconds) 2) Auto-retry of failed ad requests 3) Lifecycle management (e.g. automatically load a new ad when app is brought to foreground)
